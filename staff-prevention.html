Dengue Prevention

Protect your family and community with these essential tips

Eliminate Standing Water

Regularly check and empty containers that collect water like flower pots, tires, and buckets where mosquitoes breed.

  • Clean gutters and drains weekly
  • Cover water storage containers

Personal Protection

Protect yourself from mosquito bites day and night.

  • Use EPA-registered insect repellent
  • Wear long sleeves and pants
  • Use window screens and mosquito nets

Community Action

Work together to keep your neighborhood safe.

  • Organize weekly clean-up drives
  • Report potential breeding sites
  • Share prevention information

Dengue Symptoms

Seek medical attention immediately if you experience:

High Fever (40°C/104°F)

Sudden onset lasting 2-7 days

Severe Headache

Pain behind the eyes

Bleeding

Nose or gum bleeding, easy bruising

Warning Signs

Severe abdominal pain, persistent vomiting
